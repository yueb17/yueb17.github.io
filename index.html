About Me

I am a Research Scientist at Facebook Reality Labs (FB page). My current research interests lie in Computer Vision and Deep Learning, especially learning with limited supervision, vision & language, scene understanding and their applications for image and video understanding, AR/VR, etc.

I received my Ph.D degree from Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Yun Fu. Please find the webpage of our lab here SmileLab. I have also spent time at Google Research, Adobe Research, Bytedance AI Lab U.S. and Siemens Research as a research intern.
